How to Use

The gold plane sweeps upward as Profit increases.

The colored surface (purple → yellow) shows Profit lifted above the feasible region.

The white dotted line on the floor is the isoprofit level curve — all feasible mixes with the same profit value.

Watch which extreme point the plane last touches — that is the optimal solution.
